> **PROMPT PARA GENERAR LA IMAGEN DE PORTADA**
> 
> Usá este prompt en Midjourney, DALL-E 3 o Leonardo AI:

---

**Prompt principal (Midjourney / DALL-E 3):**

```
A professional Latin American woman in her 40s, thoughtful and warm, sitting at a minimalist wooden desk in a softly lit modern office. She's looking slightly off-camera with a calm, confident expression. Natural window light from the left creates soft shadows. On the desk: an open notebook and a small plant. Background is blurred with warm cream and off-white tones. Editorial portrait photography, Canon 85mm f/1.4, shallow depth of field, film grain, muted color grading — cream (#f5f5f0), deep charcoal (#0a0a0a), subtle violet accent light (#7c3aed). Horizontal 16:9 composition. Authentic, human, no stock photo feel.
```

**Versión corta para DALL-E 3:**

```
Editorial portrait of a professional Latin American woman at a minimal desk, warm natural light, soft cream and charcoal tones with subtle purple accent, thoughtful expression, shallow depth of field, 16:9 format, cinematic quality.
```

**Parámetros para Midjourney:**

```
--ar 16:9 --style raw --q 2 --v 6.1
```

---

Guardá la imagen generada en `public/images/posts/draft-nuevo-post/` y actualizá el campo `coverImage` del frontmatter con la ruta correspondiente antes de publicar.
